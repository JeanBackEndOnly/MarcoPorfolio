ABOUT ME

My Journey as a Full-Stack Developer

When I started coding, my focus was solely on backend development with PHP. I loved
building the logic behind web applications, focusing on database management and
server-side programming. However, during my academic journey, I was pushed to
explore frontend development, balancing new skills like CSS and HTML while strengthening
my backend expertise.

One of my most significant experiences was developing a Progressive Web App (PWA) for a
gym management system. This project taught me how to combine practicality with user
experience effectively.

Currently, I’m diving into hosting and deploying websites, aiming to create seamless
platforms. My skills include PHP, CSS, HTML, SQL, and I’m working to enhance my proficiency
in JavaScript. This portfolio marks the start of my journey to refine my craft and build
remarkable projects.
